## What is FieldOpt?

FieldOpt is a modern dispatch console that helps manage field technicians efficiently. It provides:

- **Real-time job assignment** — Drag-and-drop technician scheduling
- **Skill-based routing** — Match jobs to techs based on capabilities
- **Geographic mapping** — Visualize job locations and optimize routes
- **Advanced timeline** — See technician workload across the day
- **Data-driven dispatch** — ML-powered routing (coming v0.0.8)
